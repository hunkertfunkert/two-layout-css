go to good christmas

santa claus is coming to town!

"he sees you when you're sleeping! he knows when you're awake!"

its just about that time of year again. santa is on his way and he's brining lots of surprises this year. you'd better hope that you're on the nice list, or he'll have something entirely different in store for you. after all, he knows if you've been bad or good, so be good for goodness sakes

many residents all over the world have reported the same phenomenon: a visitation in the dead of night. only if everyone is asleep, of course. you definitely wouldn't want. to mess up the opportunity, he only comes around once a year

all i know for sure is that i will be keeping an eye out this holiday season. make sure to spend lots of time with your loved ones, and don't get caught out in the cold too long!
